Best practices và template prompt cho từng loại lỗi

Lỗi 1: Chuyển động nhóm đối tượng sai

Case: TLNOV14022rev4 - Tuần lộc tách đàn, đứng sai vị trí

Prompt sai

Santa's reindeer flying over the rooftop with sleigh

Prompt tối ưu Kling 2.6 Pro

[SCENE] Santa Claus riding a sleigh pulled by a team of reindeer flying across the night sky above a snow-covered rooftop. [MOTION CONSTRAINTS] - All reindeer MUST maintain V-formation throughout - Reindeer spacing: consistent 1-meter gap between each - Movement direction: unified left-to-right trajectory - No individual reindeer breaks away from formation - Sleigh follows directly behind the lead reindeer [CAMERA] Fixed wide shot, no movement, no shake. [NEGATIVE] Avoid: reindeer separating, reindeer behind sleigh, broken formation, erratic individual movement.
Workflow: Kling 2.6 Pro (motion control) → KlingO1 Edit (fix nếu còn lệch nhẹ)

Lỗi 2: Trang phục sai ngữ cảnh

Case: NHJAN13010 - Đàn ông mặc bikini thay vì quần bơi

Prompt sai

Man swimming in the pool wearing swimwear

Prompt tối ưu Kling 2.6 Pro

[SUBJECT] Adult Caucasian man, athletic build, standing by poolside. [CLOTHING - EXPLICIT] Wearing: men's knee-length swim trunks / board shorts Color: solid navy blue or black Style: casual beach/pool appropriate for adult male [ABSOLUTE RESTRICTIONS] NOT wearing: bikini, speedo, brief-style swimwear, women's swimwear, revealing clothing. [ACTION] Natural relaxed pose, one hand on hip, slight smile. [CAMERA] Medium shot, fixed angle, eye level.
Nguyên tắc: Chỉ định CỤ THỂ loại trang phục + Mô tả màu sắc, kiểu dáng + Nêu rõ giới tính phù hợp + Thêm negative prompt loại trừ

Lỗi 3: Hành vi người không phù hợp

Case: MNJAN2001 - Người trong bếp cử chỉ gợi cảm

Prompt tối ưu Veo 3.1

[SCENE] Modern open-concept kitchen, daytime, warm lighting. [SUBJECTS] - Person A: Woman, 35-40 years old, standing at counter - Person B: Man, 35-40 years old, standing near stove [ACTIONS - SPECIFIC] Person A: chopping vegetables on cutting board, eyes focused on hands, slight smile. Person B: stirring pot with wooden spoon, occasionally glancing at Person A with friendly expression. [BODY LANGUAGE CONSTRAINTS] - Maintain 2-feet distance between subjects - No physical contact - Hands occupied with cooking tasks - Posture: upright, casual, task-focused - Eye contact: brief, friendly, not prolonged [MOOD] Family-friendly, casual cooking session, wholesome domestic atmosphere. [NEGATIVE] Avoid: romantic gestures, suggestive poses, lingering eye contact, physical touching, leaning toward each other intimately.

Lỗi 4: Logic chuyển động vật thể sai

Case: LIJAN07001 - Quân cờ nhảy đánh nhau thay vì đi theo luật

Prompt tối ưu Nano Banana Pro

[SCENE] Overhead view of wooden chessboard with ongoing game. [CHESS MOVEMENT SEQUENCE - EXPLICIT] Move 1: White pawn E2 slides forward to E4 (2 squares) Duration: 1.5 seconds, Pause: 1 second Move 2: Black pawn D7 slides forward to D5 (2 squares) Duration: 1.5 seconds, Pause: 1 second Move 3: White bishop F1 slides diagonally to B5 Duration: 2 seconds [MOVEMENT RULES] - ONE piece moves at a time, others completely still - Pieces SLIDE smoothly along board surface - No jumping (except knights) - No floating or lifting off board - No piece disappears or duplicates [NEGATIVE] Avoid: pieces fighting, pieces jumping randomly, multiple pieces moving simultaneously.

Lỗi 5: Nguồn nước xuất hiện sai vị trí

Case: DUJAN04005 - Nước chảy ngẫu nhiên, không từ vòi phun

Prompt tối ưu Veo 3.1

[SCENE] Stone fountain barrel/basin in garden setting, currently empty, daylight. [WATER SOURCE - EXPLICIT] Water emerges ONLY from the fountain spout/nozzle at the TOP CENTER of the barrel structure. [WATER BEHAVIOR] - Water streams downward from spout - Falls naturally into basin below - Creates realistic splash on impact - Ripples spread outward from impact point - Water level gradually rises in basin [PHYSICS CONSTRAINTS] - Water obeys gravity: flows DOWN only - No water appearing from sides of barrel - No water materializing mid-air - Flow rate: steady, moderate stream [NEGATIVE] Avoid: water from nowhere, water from walls, floating water, reverse gravity, dry spout.

Lỗi 6: Chuyển mùa sai context

Case: TADEC31004 - Làm tuyết→xuân thay vì hiện trạng→xuân

Prompt tối ưu Nano Banana Pro

[STARTING STATE] Current condition of the house exterior: - Dry grass, brownish-yellow lawn - Bare trees with no leaves - Overcast sky - NO SNOW (this location does not have snow) [ENDING STATE] Spring transformation: - Lush green grass covering lawn - Trees full of fresh green leaves - Flowers blooming across garden - Blue sky with soft clouds [CRITICAL CONSTRAINT] - START with current DRY condition - DO NOT add snow at any point - This is a warm climate location [NEGATIVE] Avoid: snow, ice, frost, winter elements.
Context Checklist: Khách hàng ở vùng khí hậu nào? Có tuyết không? Mùa hiện tại trong ảnh là gì? Mùa mục tiêu là gì?

Lỗi 7: Mặt người biến dạng

Case: HTJAN07002rev2 - Deformed faces

Prompt chống lỗi mặt Kling 2.6 Pro

[SUBJECT FACE PROTECTION] - Maintain consistent facial structure throughout - Eyes: symmetrical, natural blink rate - Mouth: natural movement, no morphing - Skin texture: consistent, no artifacts - Face proportions: stable, no warping [MOTION LIMITS FOR FACES] - Head rotation: maximum 30 degrees - No rapid head movements - Subtle expressions only [NEGATIVE] Avoid: face melting, asymmetrical features, extra limbs, distorted proportions.
Workflow: Kling 2.6 Pro với face protection → Review từng 1-2 giây → KlingO1 Edit fix local nếu lỗi nhẹ → Re-generate segment nếu lỗi nặng

Lỗi 8: Video tĩnh, thiếu chuyển động

Case: THJAN20030Rev - Trời xanh như ảnh tĩnh zoom vào

Prompt tối ưu Veo 3.1

[SKY REPLACEMENT] Transform overcast sky to vibrant blue sky. [SKY DYNAMICS - ESSENTIAL] - Soft white clouds drifting slowly left-to-right - Cloud shadows moving gently across landscape - Birds flying in distant background (2-3 birds) [ENVIRONMENTAL RESPONSE] - Tree leaves rustling slightly in breeze - Grass blades swaying gently - Soft shadows shifting on ground [CAMERA] Fixed position, NO zoom, NO dolly. Only environmental elements move. [NEGATIVE] Avoid: static sky, frozen clouds, no environmental movement, Ken Burns effect only.
Anti-Static Rule: Thêm cloud movement + leaf/grass rustle + shadow shift + distant birds. KHÔNG chỉ zoom vào ảnh tĩnh.

Master Prompt Template

[SCENE DESCRIPTION] → Bối cảnh, thời gian, ánh sáng [SUBJECTS] → Người/vật, đặc điểm cụ thể, vị trí [ACTIONS/MOTION] → Hành động CHI TIẾT, timing, sequence [CONSTRAINTS] → Giới hạn vật lý, logic, phải tuân thủ [CAMERA] → Góc quay, movement, stability [NEGATIVE PROMPT] → Những gì KHÔNG ĐƯỢC xuất hiện
